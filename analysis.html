Investigation 01

Null SIC Code Report

Week 36-44 2025 • Sample: 200 Contacts

Parked Chats65.5%

Executive Summary

Identified two root causes for null SIC codes: 131 parked chats auto-resolving without codes and 69 instances of manual agent failure. 80.4% of phone-related missing codes were behavioral rather than technical defects.

Strategic Actions

Implementing automated SIC assignment for parked chats and mandatory UI controls for agents. Collaborating with international stakeholders (Amazon India) to scale 100% resolution.
